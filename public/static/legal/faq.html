FAQ

GENERAL

What is WAYA and how does it work?

WAYA is Nigeria’s new digital wallet. WAYA also allows you to send money directly to the smartphones of your friends and family. Whether you are shopping, paying for a meal in a restaurant, wanting to make a purchase from a vending machine or browsing an online shop: With WAYA you can make convenient and secure payments using your smartphone.

What benefits does WAYA offer?

WAYA offers lots of benefits like;

  • Easy and convenient means of sending and receiving money from friends/family with or without a smartphone.
  • Make simple payments to merchants or pay bills on the go.
  • Enjoy chats, calls and resource sharing with the WAYA Android and iOS smartphones apps.
  • Even when you’re out of data, WAYA offers you a well-structured SMS based transaction functionalities with USSD code.
  • WAYA offers a social media platform called WayaGram just for you to share your moments and posts with friends/family without leaving the app.

Which smartphones and phones does WAYA work with?

WAYA mobile app works on all iOS (from iPhone 5 upwards) and Android (from Android 6.0 upwards) smartphones while USSD code transactions can be done using any smartphone or none-smartphone with SMS/text message capability.

Which telecommunications providers support WAYA?

WAYA works with all telecommunication providers in Nigeria.

What does it cost to download WAYA?

You can download the WAYA app FREE of charge from the app stores.

Where can I update my personal details?

You can update your personal details under the menu item “Profile”> “Settings” > “Profile Settings” > “Edit Details”. If you change your details, WAYA will verify your identify once more.

I have forgotten my WAYA Password. What can I do?

Select “Forgotten Password?” beneath the WAYA password entry field. We will send One-Time-Password (OTP) to your registered WAYA phone number or email address. Then enter the OTP on field provided for you to access the change of password page.

Where can I find information on the General Terms and Conditions?

You can find the General Terms and Conditions and information on the Added-Value Services (“Third-party offers”) under the menu item “Profile” > “Settings” > “About” > “Conditions of use”. Or on www.wayapaychat.com.

I have lost my smartphone. What can I do?

Please contact the WAYA customer support at info@wayapaychat.com. You will not lose your money, as it is not saved directly on your smartphone.

Why does WAYA want authorization to access my camera or contacts, for example?

WAYA require access to your camera during the scanning of a QR code, video calls or when you would like to attach a photo when sending money. WAYA uses the access to your contacts in connection with the ‘Send money’ function. This allows for your contact list to be displayed in the app.

Does WAYA disclose my data to third parties?

WAYA never discloses individual data to providers and/or third parties. Exception: If you provide your express consent, for example upon saving a customer loyalty card in the WAYA app. This solution has been reviewed with the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) and been rated as exemplary.

Does WAYA use my usage data and profile information?

WAYA only uses this data if you have expressly confirmed your desire in the app to receive third-party offers. This solution has been reviewed with the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) and been rated as exemplary. If you have provided your consent, WAYA will use your usage data and profile information in order to enable it to display offers to you in the app that are appropriate for you.

TOP UP MY WAYA WALLET

How can I top up my wallet in the WAYA app?

You can top up your wallet through the following ways:

  • Via your bank account
  • Via your bank card
  • Via USSD
  • Via bank transfer.

What is the maximum amount I can top up?

You can top up your wallet as much as the daily transfer limit of your chosen bank. This limit differs from bank to bank however, you can extend the transfer limit of your account on signed consent with your chosen bank.

Can I add several bank accounts or bank cards in WAYA?

Yes, you can connect several bank accounts or bank cards in WAYA

Can I connect a foreign bank account?

No, you can only connect a Nigerian bank account in WAYA

How can I withdraw my money?

You can transfer your money from the WAYA wallet back to your bank account. For legal reasons, it is only possible for withdrawals to be transferred to an account belonging to you.

How it works:

Open the WAYA app. From the menu select “Waya Pay” > select “Wallet” > select “Balance” > select “Withdraw Money”, confirm bank account you are withdrawing to, enter the amount to withdraw and then select “Withdraw to Bank Account”.

How much can I withdraw from my wallet?

You can withdraw as much as you have in your wallet.

SEND AND REQUEST MONEY

Can I send or request money from someone if he/she do not have WAYA account?

Yes, it is possible to send money to somebody even if they do not yet have WAYA account. The receiver will get an SMS/text message about the transfer and how to retrieve the amount via WAYA. If the receiver chooses to open an account on WAYA, the money will be credited to his wallet automatically but if he chooses to retrieve the amount instead, he will be required to visit any WAYA merchant near him to cash the money using the retrieval code in the SMS that was sent to him. You CANNOT request money from individuals who do not have WAYA account.

Somebody has requested money from me. What can I do?

You can accept the request and send the money in the WAYA app. Or you can reject the request. Select “Activity list” in the main menu. You can accept and reject individual requests here.

Is it possible to cancel a money transfer?

No, this is generally not possible.

I’ve sent money to a wrong phone number/person or sent a wrong amount to someone. What do I do?

You need to contact and ask the person to transfer the amount back to you.

Is it possible to reject a payment request?

Yes, it is possible. You can reject any request from “Payment Requests” > “All Payment Requests” > then select the request under “Requests Sent to me” list and click “Reject” option.

PAY MERCHANTS

How is my money transferred to the WAYA app?

If you use your bank’s WAYA app, you can link WAYA to your bank account in a simple manner or save your credit card. When you make payments with WAYA, the purchase amount will be debited directly to your bank account or credit card. When you use the WAYA prepaid app you can top up your wallet from the WAYA app.

Where can I pay with WAYA?

You can make convenient payments with WAYA at cash registers and vending machines as well as in online and app shops. Acceptance points can be identified by the WAYA logo.

How can I pay with WAYA at a merchant?

Select “Waya Pay” from the WAYA app menu and then select “Pay Merchant” then select either “Scan to Pay” or “Pay via Phone”. If you select the former option, point your smartphone camera to scan the QR code that is displayed to you to make your payment. You can type in the merchant phone number and simply transfer the amount as well if you select the latter option. You will receive an SMS or in-app notification after the payment has been successfully made.

Can I pay with WAYA if I have no mobile reception?

Yes, it is possible to make offline payments without having data connection using WAYA USSD code.

Can I also pay with WAYA outside Nigeria?

It’s not possible to pay with WAYA outside Nigeria at the moment.

Can I make WAYA payments in foreign currencies?

No, you can only make payments with WAYA in Nigerian Naira.

Can I view my previous payments in the WAYA app?

Yes, you can view all your payments at any time under “Transactions”. Select “Waya Pay” from the menu list and select “Wallet” to see the Transaction option.

What are the requirements for using a QR code sticker?

Your WAYA app needs to be up to date in order to execute payments via QR code stickers. If your app isn’t up to date, you will need to download the latest version.

I scanned a QR code twice or paid a merchant twice by mistake. Who do I need to contact?

You need to ask the merchant to transfer the excess amount back to you.

What happens if I have too little credit in the WAYA app to pay for a specific amount?

If you have insufficient credit for a purchase, part of the purchase amount will be paid with WAYA. You can settle the remaining amount using another payment method.
